Semantic_Scholar

Semantic Scholar provides free, AI-driven search and discovery tools, and open resources for the global research community. It indexes over 200 million academic papers sourced from publisher partnerships, data providers, and web crawls. AI features allow to provide abstract and citation analysis.

Bibliodiversity support Semantic scholar was initially focused on computer science, geoscience, and neuroscience, and since 2017 biomedical studies. It is now open to any field of science, but the proportion of each field is not known. There doesn't seem to be any limitation related to language or geographic area.

The service is free and doesn't reference papers behind paywalls.
